Name: Poppy

Breed: Mix

Get ready to fall in love with this goofball! Poppy enjoys chasing just about everything and executing a sneak attack from around corners when you least expect it. You'll find her sleeping in the strangest positions, which she seems perfectly fine with!

Name: Ludo


Breed: Mix

Ludo is quiet and shy, but once he's warmed up to you, expect to have a lot of lap time. Ludo just wants to be your friend, and the best way he knows how is through copius cuddles.

Name: Trixie

Breed: Mix

As her name implies, Trixie is quite energetic! She zooms back and forth from room to room, and loves to play with her toys. She especially likes to play 'fetch,' so make sure you have a good throwing arm. Her forever home should have lots of kitty toys for her to explore.
