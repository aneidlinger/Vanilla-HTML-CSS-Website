About Me

Name: Adam Neidlinger

Breed: Human-mix

Doggos and Kittehs is a conceptual animal shelter that I created as the subject of the final project for my class on web design. I chose an animal shelter as the subject purely based on my love for animals. I have three pets of my own (2 cats, and 1 dog). If I had the space and the money, I'd adopt even more creatures.
